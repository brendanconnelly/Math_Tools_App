How to enter expressions

Use nCr for combinations and nPr for permutations. Standard operators +, −, * and / are supported (order of operations applies). Example: 8P3 + 10C4 * 2P2

A permutation counts ordered arrangements; a combination counts unordered selections. For the same n and r, nPr = nCr × r!
